During the summer of 2019 I had the amazing opportunity to go to a 3-day camping festival in Washington where I got to meet the members and had a flag signed.

It was one of the happiest moments of my life!

I hope to have more opportunities in the future to meet the band members and continue sharing my love for what they do.
